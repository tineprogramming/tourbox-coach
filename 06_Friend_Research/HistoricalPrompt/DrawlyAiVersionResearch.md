# Mission
Please help me research and tell me if it is technically possible to build an Ai-assisting drawing software that's kind of like Drawly.

## Purpose/Goal
The goal is make it user friendly who beginners who are new to digital drawing, so this tool can help them quickly to get their hands on drawing.  

## Ideas/Inspiration

### 1. Drawly (The Target Quality)
Drawly is getting popular recently, where people can draw with any friends online. People loves this APP. It doesn't matter you are beginner or professional art student. You can see the other's dawing in real-time, which is very fun to play.

Instead of real human, we can have Ai to accompany and draw with you. It can also draw with you in real-time. You can choose the agent and let it be your friend or art teacher. It can also chat with you while you are drawing, to make you happy or to educate you during the drawing.

#### Reference:
Link: https://getdrawly.com/

### 2. Google AutoDraw (Minimun Standard)
This should be the minimun standard of this Ai-assisting drawing software. You can draw something really simply. AutoDraw will help you auto-finish or let you choose several improved options. 

#### Additional Idea for AutoDraw
We can build stroke prediction feature, so it is kind of like Copilot for VScode, programmer just need to hit "Tab" key when they like about the AI suggested lines of code. 

In this case, you draw a few strokes → AI instantly suggests 3–5 possible next-stroke sequences (imagine like ghosted lightly on canvas, so it is intuitive to draw on it).

#### Reference:
Link: https://www.autodraw.com/

### 3. ArtWorkout
This APP aims to be the Duolingo for drawing. We all know about how great that Duolingo gamify the education learning process. We can do the samething for all the boring lessons for drawing and make it fun to follow day-by-day.

#### Reference:
Link: https://artworkout.app/
