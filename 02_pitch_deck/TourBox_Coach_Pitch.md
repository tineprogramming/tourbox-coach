**Headline:**  
AI generation isn't the answer.  
*AI tutoring is.*

### ❌ AI Generation (left, muted)
*"Make me a beautiful image"*

- No progression, no growth

### ✓ AI Tutoring (right, dark purple — premium)
*"Show me how to draw, step by step"*

- Hardware-software combo = defensible moat

**Closer:** *Beginners don't want shortcuts. They want to grow.*

## Slide 10 — Parallel Track: Pro Ecosystem ⚡ NEW

**Section:** 09 • PARALLEL TRACK

**Headline:** We don't abandon our pros — we level them up too.

**Two-column layout:**

### Why this matters
While we build the beginner platform, our 300K existing pro customers get an upgrade path through software — at minimal additional cost.

This protects revenue, deepens loyalty, and signals to the market that TourBox is serious about AI across the entire creative spectrum.

### What we ship (parallel to AI Tutor)

| Track | Investment | Outcome |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| **TourBox AI Bridge app** | 2 engineers, 4 mo | Routes TourBox actions to local AI tools |
| **ComfyUI custom node** | 1 engineer, 2 mo | Viral with AI art community (~500K users) |
| **Krita / Photoshop plugins** | 2 engineers, 6 mo | Pro workflows with Stable Diffusion, Flux |
| **Open SDK + marketplace** | Year 2 | Community-built plugins, 30% revenue share |

**Bottom callout:** *Pro track total cost: ~$600K over 12 months. Parallel team, no impact on AI Tutor timeline.*

`[SPEAKER]` "This is our hedge. If beginner segment surprises us, pro track keeps the ship moving. And it costs less than 5% of the total program. Strategically, it's free insurance."
