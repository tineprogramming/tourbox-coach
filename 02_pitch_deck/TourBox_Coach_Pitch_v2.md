## Slide 5 — Product Vision

**Section:** 04 • PRODUCT VISION

**Headline:** A personal art tutor in a box.

**Persona callout (top, small):**

> *Meet Mai, 14. She wants to draw anime but is afraid to post anywhere. YouTube is overwhelming. Her parents won't pay for tutors. TourBox Coach is for her.*

**Four feature cards (2×2 grid, white icon in purple circle):**

### 🧭 Curated Curriculum
100+ progressive lessons — from first line to finished portrait. Co-designed with working artists.

### ⚙️ Ghost Guide Dial — *the magic moment*
The **muscle memory controller**. Turn the knob to set AI assistance: 100% (guided trace) → 50% (faded reference) → 0% (you're drawing solo).

### 👁️ AI Sight Feedback
After each stroke, vision AI reviews proportion, line confidence, and suggests one specific improvement. Encouraging, never critical.

### 🔒 Private by Default
All AI runs on-device. No cloud, no data leaves the device. Parents trust it for kids.

`[SPEAKER]` "The Ghost Guide Dial is our killer feature. A beginner traces at 100%, then over weeks dials down to 0%. The hardware physically embodies the learning journey. No app can replicate this."

## Slide 6 — Why TourBox Wins

**Section:** 05 • WHY TOURBOX WINS THIS

**Headline:** Our unfair advantage: the **Haptic Brain**

**Hero statement:**

> Skill-building requires **physical touch** — not just a touchscreen.  
> We already shipped the body. Now we add the brain.

**Four advantages (stacked rows, big numbers on left):**

**01 — 7 years of tactile IP**  
Patents on the multi-control layout, dial mechanics, and haptic feedback. Software-only competitors cannot replicate the *feel* of a real dial.

**02 — Two-hand creative workflow**  
Apple Pencil owns the right hand. Nobody owns the left. TourBox is the only product designed for left-hand control while drawing — the ergonomic insight Apple, Wacom, and Adobe missed.

**03 — 300K loyal customer base**  
Built-in beta pool, social proof, and brand trust. Day-one validation pool no startup can match.

**04 — Edge-AI sweet spot, right now**  
Mobile-class chips (Snapdragon, Dimensity) hit the inflection: 30+ TOPS at $80 BOM. The window opened in 2025. It closes when everyone notices.

**Closer:** *Competitors need 5 years to build our supply chain and IP. We just need 12 months to add the brain.*

`[SPEAKER]` "Three of these four advantages are uncopyable. The fourth — edge AI cost — is a 12-month moat. After that, the chip is commoditized. We need to move now."

## Killer demo to bring

**The Ghost Guide Dial demo (most important):**
- Show a 30-sec clip: someone draws a face
- Knob at 100%: full reference shown, beginner traces
- Knob at 50%: reference fades, harder
- Knob at 0%: gone, beginner draws independently
- *This is the "magic moment" — CEO needs to see it, not just hear about it*

If you can't film, do paper sketch with overlay transparency layers.

## Privacy emphasis (huge in 2026)

Whenever AI comes up, hit one of these:
- "All processing on-device. Nothing leaves the box."
- "No child's drawing ever touches our servers."
- "Parents won't buy AI for kids unless it's private. We are private."
